      Internet

    1. I have been online since 1994. I have spent thousands of hours, since the Summer of 2001, learning the basics of web page design.       3. Numistatics

    3. The study and collection of coins and currency.I collect coins and currency for both their historical and investment value. The U.S. Mint and Bureau of Engraving and Printing have created many programs to collect coins, currency and learn a lot about American History. Throughout the course of the school year, I try to incorporate many coins from my collection into my lesson plans. This gives my students the opportunity to see first-hand how coins have changed throughout American history.
